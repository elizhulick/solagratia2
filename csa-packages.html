Our CSA

Members in Sola Gratia Farm will receive fresh, seasonal vegetables from the farm weekly during the growing season. We will be growing 125 varieties of over 50 different crops this year. All crops are grown following organic methods. Produce is distributed weekly. Add-on shares are available, including bread from Rick's Bakery and mushrooms from Flyway Family Farm. Ala carte sales of honey from Second Nature Honey, coffee from Luna Verde and fruit from local farms available seasonally.

*We will not be offering Spring shares this year, instead please shop from our market stand!
